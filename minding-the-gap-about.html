About the project

Our audience

Parents and school administrators in Montgomery and Prince George's Counties.

One way that we would distribute this project to reach our target audience is an online promotion campaign where we share our project with bloggers, Facebook forums and other prominent community members/groups (some examples: Parents Coalition of Montgomery County, Montgomery County MD Neighbors for Local Schools, PG Parents for Education, Parents for PGCPS). By sharing our work with these activists, parents, advocates and local school board members and representatives, we would encourage them to amplify the message by using their own channels and pages to share it with a broader group of people who follow them on social media.
